🍽️ Happy Life – Modern Restaurant & Cafe Website

A premium, modern, and fully responsive restaurant website designed for Happy Life Restaurant & Cafe in Jamjodhpur, Gujarat. The website combines elegant visuals, smooth animations, and a luxury dining experience to attract customers and increase online orders.

📌 Project Overview

Happy Life is a modern restaurant landing page that blends contemporary design with traditional culinary culture. The website showcases the restaurant's menu, customer reviews, brand story, and contact information while providing direct WhatsApp ordering functionality.

The design focuses on creating a premium dining experience through elegant typography, dark luxury aesthetics, and smooth user interactions.
